WindMind

In collaboration with engineers without borders, as a final project in the course Information Visualization, we present to you WindMind. WindMind is a dashboard visualization created to aid egineers working in Tanzania to maintain windturbines used to charge batteries in rural areas.

About the project

Our mission: To assist engineers in Tanzania to effectively maintain and repair wind turbines by providing them with a suitable visualization tool.

Problems faced by the tanzanian engineers: long distances to maintain turbines, gathering information about turbine and the lack of maintenances records

Goals for this project: quick and effective access to data and visualizations,remote monitoring capability, maintenance log with long history and simplified communication between engineers and on site.
